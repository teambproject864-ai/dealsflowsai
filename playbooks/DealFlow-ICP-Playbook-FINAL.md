# DealFlow AI - Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) Playbook
## Playbook Version: 2.0 | Last Updated: 2026-06-11
---

## 1. Executive Summary
This Ideal Customer Profile Playbook is DealFlow AI’s strategic guide for targeting, prioritizing, and converting high-value B2B SaaS companies into customers. Our primary ICP consists of growth-stage B2B SaaS businesses that need AI-powered lead analysis, meeting automation, and sales performance analytics. By focusing exclusively on this ICP, DealFlow AI will achieve:
- 40% higher conversion rates
- 60% higher customer lifetime value (LTV)
- 35% lower customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Faster sales cycles and increased team efficiency

---

## 2. ICP Definition & Attributes
### Core ICP Definition
DealFlow AI’s ideal customer is a **growth-stage B2B SaaS company (10-250 employees)** with a dedicated sales team, using modern tools like CRMs, video conferencing, and LinkedIn Sales Navigator, seeking to automate their sales process, prioritize high-quality leads, and improve sales performance through AI-driven insights.

### Non-Ideal Leads (Exclusion Criteria)
- Freelancers and solopreneurs
- B2C companies
- Early-stage startups (<$500k ARR) with no sales team
- Enterprise companies (>1000 employees) requiring extensive custom onboarding
- Companies not using CRMs or video conferencing tools

## 10. Cross-Team Alignment Guidelines
### Sales Team
- Allocate 80% of effort to Tier 1-2 accounts
- Use standardized ICP-aligned demo scripts
- Update lead scores in real-time
- Prioritize hot leads within 15 minutes
- Use the validation checklist before every demo

### Marketing Team
- 100% of content tailored to at least one ICP segment
- 85% of ad spend prioritized for high-LTV segments
- Nurture sequences mapped to buying journey stages
- Coordinate with product to create ICP-specific case studies
- Track channel ROI monthly and adjust budget

### Customer Success Team
- Identify upsell opportunities within first 60 days
- Use playbook to identify at-risk accounts
- Collect case studies from high-fit customers
- Conduct ICP-aligned quarterly business reviews
- Create reference materials for sales/marketing

### Product Team
- Use ICP insights to prioritize roadmap
- Build integrations based on ICP tech stacks
- Test new features with ICP-aligned beta users
- Gather feedback from VP Sales, Sales Ops, and sales reps

## 11. ICP Validation Checklist
Use this checklist for every new lead or account to verify alignment:

- [ ] Is it a B2B SaaS company?
- [ ] Employee count between 10-250? (Or target ICP tier)
- [ ] ≥ $500k ARR?
- [ ] Uses CRM (Salesforce/HubSpot)?
- [ ] Uses video conferencing (Zoom/Google Meet)?
- [ ] Has a dedicated sales team (≥3 reps)?
- [ ] Located in target geography (NA/EU)?
- [ ] Has identified pain points that DealFlow AI solves?
- [ ] Has budget for SaaS tools ($5k-$50k annual)?
- [ ] Sales team actively researching automation tools?

**Qualification Thresholds**:
- Pass: ≥ 7/10 boxes checked → Hot/Warm Lead
- Fail: < 5 boxes checked → Not ICP aligned
