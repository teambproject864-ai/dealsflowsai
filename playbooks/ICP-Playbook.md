## Executive Summary
This Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) Playbook provides standardized, data-driven guidance for DealFlow AI’s go-to-market teams (Sales, Marketing, Customer Success) to prioritize high-value prospects, deliver personalized messaging, and optimize conversion rates. Our primary ICP is defined as growth-stage B2B SaaS companies seeking AI-powered lead analysis and meeting automation tools to scale their sales operations.

---

## Core ICP Definition & Attributes
### Primary ICP
- **Company Type**: Growth-stage B2B SaaS (10-250 employees)
- **Primary Use Case**: Lead prioritization, meeting automation, and AI-powered call summaries
- **Technology Stack**: Uses Salesforce/HubSpot, Zoom/Google Meet, LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- **Geography**: North America (primary), EU/UK (secondary)
- **Budget Range**: $5k-$50k ARR (annual recurring revenue)

### Secondary ICP
- **Company Type**: Enterprise B2B companies (250+ employees) in tech, fintech, or healthcare
- **Primary Use Case**: Full sales cycle automation and team performance analytics
- **Budget Range**: $50k-$250k ARR

## Pain Point Analysis
### Table 2: Pain Point Severity & Impact Assessment
| Pain Point | Severity | Business Impact | DealFlow AI Solution Alignment |
|------------|----------|-----------------|--------------------------------|
| Time spent manually qualifying leads | Critical | 20-40 hours/week wasted per sales rep | AI-powered lead analysis scores leads instantly |
| No visibility into call quality & performance | High | Missed coaching opportunities, inconsistent messaging | Meeting summaries, sentiment analysis, performance dashboards |
| Difficulty prioritizing outreach | Critical | Low conversion rates, wasted effort on low-fit prospects | ICP matching, deal health scoring |
| Lack of meeting automation | Medium | Repetitive scheduling tasks, no follow-ups | One-click AI meeting assistant, auto-generated next steps |
| Limited data for sales strategy | Medium | Reactive vs. proactive sales | Analytics & insights for coaching, forecasting |

## Cross-Team Alignment Guidelines
### Sales Team
- Focus 80% of effort on Tier 1-2 accounts
- Use ICP-aligned demo scripts
- Update lead scores in real-time

### Marketing Team
- Content tailored to each ICP segment
- Ad spend prioritized to high-LTV segments
- Nurture sequences aligned to buying journey

### Customer Success Team
- Identify expansion opportunities early
- Use playbook to identify at-risk accounts
- Collect case studies from high-fit customers
